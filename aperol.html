Italian Aperol Spritz Recipe

Description

We’re going to Italy this summer, so I’m celebrating with this classic Aperol spritz recipe! Aperol spritzes are ultra refreshing, not-too-sweet bubbly cocktails.

This is a classic cocktail that you will definetely enjoy if you like something refreshing to drink after a meal.

Ingredients

  • Aperol
  • Prosecco
  • Club Soda
  • Slice of fresh orange
  • Ice

Steps

  1. You’ll simply fill a wine glass with ice, then add Aperol, Prosecco, club soda and a slice of orange.
  2. Enjoy!
